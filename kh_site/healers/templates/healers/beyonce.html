Dance like Beyoncé

Join me in an actual dance studio to re-create some of Beyoncé’s classic choreography!

 

We’ll be going through the same choreography for four meetups in a row then moving onto another of Beyonce’s fabulous choreography. Using tutorials of Beyoncé’s choreography from YouTube (or whatever other videos) together we will learn the choreography.

 

Although I am a dancer, please trust that I will be grappling with the movements myself so this group is for those people comfortable with working together. Please bring $8 to help cover the cost of the studio.

Come prepared to laugh at yourself, access your fierce alter ego, and do a lot of moving!
