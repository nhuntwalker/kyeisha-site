Healing the Healers

The Stress of Serving Those with Trauma

Listening to sometimes detailed stories of despair and bearing witnesses to the pain, fear, and terror that trauma survivors have endured can leave negative consequences. Healing professionals who do this sacred work can find themselves experiencing burnout, vicarious trauma, and compassion fatigue.

In this stressful social climate the demand for healing professionals has risen and with it a need for us to intentionally practice self-care. However self-care still remains elusive, this led to the creation of Healing the Healers (HTH). Through workshops, and 1-on-1 or team coaching our services empower healers to live a quality of life that reflects their brilliance and strength.

Healing the Healers

HTH assists helping professionals:

  • Identify and practice self-care techniques unique to them

  • Cultivate exceptional introspective skills

  • Learn to use introspection as the foundation of personal and professional excellence

  • Challenge feelings of helplessness and hopelessness that can be common when helping trauma survivors

  • Identify the challenges, strengths, and weaknesses associated with being a healing professional

  • Understand the warning signs and prevent the onset of burnout

  • Improve their overall quality of life, including developing a consistent PLAY practice

  • Learn to clear the emotional residue of others as a self-care tool

Benefits that participants report:

  • Decreased potential for burnout and improvement in mental health

  • Innovation and creativity in workplace and in personal life

  • A stronger sense of self and purpose

  • Energy and mental space to focus on priorities (including work)

  • Ability to establish and maintain boundaries

  • Enhancement of decision-making skills

  • Enhancement of job performance

  • Improvement of overall quality of life

Workshops

Healing the Healers has given self-care workshops to case managers and other helping professionals at organizations like the YWCA, Rainier Valley Corps, Seattle Therapy Alliance, and Wonder of Women Inc. These workshops give participants the opportunity to assess their current self care as well as acquire tools to immediately begin improving their self care and therefore the quality of their lives. Please contact me here if you wish to learn more.

Self Care Mastery Coaching

Our services also support organizations and individuals by offering one-on-one and team coaching. HTH will contract with organizations to meet with their staff individually and within their teams. This role is known as the Trauma Stewardship Coach which is based off of the powerful book by Laura van Dernoot Lipsky titled "Trauma Stewardship: An Everyday Guide to Caring for Self While Caring for Others". It's an inspirational book that every Healer should read. Click here to learn more.
