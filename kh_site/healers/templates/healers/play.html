Healers at Play

As adults we tend to leave the playing to the little ones. Yet, after studying and surveying helping professionals in the community it appears that we need to learn a thing or two from the kiddos!

Healing the Healers works to support the average helping professional in identifying, engaging in, and developing a practice of play!

Before founding Healing the Healers, I could still attest to the impact of play in my own life. I demanded time in my week to let loose and as a dancer, a movie enthusiast, and a performer arts supporter, I found ways to fill my weeks with fun and non-therapy speak.
